About

What's the purpose of this website?

Skatboarding is a big passion & hobby of mine. Just because skatboarding is a fun activity/sport, doesn't mean it hasn't surprsingly taught me important life lessons. It has taught me that repetition is key & hard work, does in fact, pay off. When you're grinding for hours, weeks, months, even years for a trick & finally land it, is a feeling like no other. I plan to transfer this lesson throughout any goal/ activity I do in my life.
