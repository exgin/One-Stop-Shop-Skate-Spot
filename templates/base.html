Learn How To Skate

No matter what age you are, I personally believe you can learn how to skateboard. There are many resources available online to help you! Here are some basic tricks to get you started.

Pushing

• Let's start off with the foundation, pushing! Pushing allows the skatboarder to actually move. You place all of your weight on your prefered foot (right = goofy, left = regular), and push off the opposite foot. It takes practice.

Lean More!

Shuv-it

• Shuv-it's are the upmost easiest trick to learn. Anyone can learn this trick within in a day. It's usually done in grass for beginners to overcome commitment issues, but after you get the hang of it, its just a shuv-it!

Learn More!

Manual

• This is a trick that I wish I spent more time on originally learning. Seeing someone hold a manual for a long time is something else. All a manual is, is balancing on the back trucks of your skateboard, whilst the front srping in the air! For more advanced 'technical' skaters, trick is flipped-into, flipped-out-of, held for a long time, just be creative with it!

Learn More!

Hippie Jump

• Probably one of the most basic, straight-foward tricks, the hippie jump. Hippie jump is when you jump on-&-off your board. It's that simple! But simple doesn't mean easy. With hippie jumps, or anything in skating, all you have to do is get over the fear of not committing to a trick.

Learn More!

Ollie

• The bread-&-butter staple trick for any skater, the ollie. The ollie opens an extensive amount of tricks in skating. New skaters spend months & years perfecting this trick. Once you have a good ollie, the sky is the limit. It extends into the most known skatboarding trick, the kickflip. So, go out there & practice!
